Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious recipe originating from Italy. Proper food for the whole family.

If you are lactose-intolerant, be sure to use an appropriate cheese on top!

Ingredients

  • Pasta plates
  • Ground beef - 400g
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cheese

How do I make it?

  1. Cook the ground beef in a pan or pot. Season it with salt and pepper, and whatever else you wish to add.
  2. Add the tomato sauce.
  3. Pour the beef mix into a casserole, then add a layer of pasta plates. Repeat until you're out of pasta plates, then add a beef layer on top.
  4. Finish the dish by spreading a layer of cheese on top.
  5. Let it cook in the oven for 30 minutes.
