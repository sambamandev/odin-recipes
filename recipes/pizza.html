Pizza

Description

Everybody loves pizza. This one is especially delicious. Unique flavor. And it's easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

How do I make it?

  1. Add the flour, oil and water together to make the dough.
  2. Let the dough swell for 1 hour.
  3. Put the dough at your counter and create a flat circle shape.
  4. Add an even layer of tomato sauce.
  5. Add the cheese.
  6. Spread the pepperonis on top!
  7. Cook it in the oven on 220C degrees for 15 minutes.
