Spaghetti

Description

A classic in the Italian kitchen. Spaghetti goes way back. And it also tastes good.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

How do I make it?

  1. Let the spaghetti cook in boiling water for about 10 minutes.
  2. Do a taste-test to see if it's been cooked properly.
  3. Add some oil to the spaghetti. This ensures that it doesn't get sticky.
  4. Present the spaghetti on a plate.
  5. Add seasoning (salt and pepper) to the spaghetti.
